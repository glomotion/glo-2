aurorae

created in the essence of aurorae (collisions between charged particles & atoms in the thermosphere), glo is designed to explore new/future web technology & how it collides with modern ui/ux practise (and my own creative interface ideas).

welcome. my name is tim paul, and I am a passionate ux/ui + motion designer currently living in sydney, nsw.

a little about tim

After over ten years in the design industry, I think of myself as a hardworking, loyal and reliable person, a highly skilled team member, and an exemplary team leader.

I relate to people well and work hard to achieve the goals I set for myself.

I'm originally from Perth WA and moved over to Sydney in late 2011. I work for p.ota.to onsite @ Google Sydney and help make cool prototype web things based on Google tech. Some of it, I'm allowed to show off - some not so much … :)

I'm a UI/UX Designer, though nowadays I also help a fellow potato build alot of what I design. You can go here to read more about my skills
